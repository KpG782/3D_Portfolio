What the mark should communicate

TeamOS is one private system for daily operations.

The logo should not only look technical. It should help someone quickly understand that TeamOS combines the work that used to be split across several tools.

Projects

Kanban, sprints, comments, and activity feeds.

Attendance

Live presence and overtime approvals.

Hiring

ATS, resume parsing, and talent pool consent.

Learning

Courses, quizzes, cohorts, and PDF certificates.

AI review

Executive briefings and drafts sent to human queues.

Recommended direction: Team Hub. It is the easiest to understand because the center reads as TeamOS and the surrounding blocks represent the separate tools it replaced.
